---
title: "Editing Cards"
weight: 70
---

Long-press any card to open its context menu:

- **Add to Favorites / Remove from Favorites** — toggles the Quick Access carousel pin
- **Edit** — opens the same form you used to add the card
- **Delete** — confirms first, then permanently removes the card

![Context menu over a card showing favorite/edit/delete options](/scandio/manual/08-context-menu.png)

Deletes are immediate and there's no Trash, so the confirmation is your safety net. If you keep regular [backups](../backup-and-restore/), a deleted card can be restored from a `.scandiobackup` file.
