Scandio keeps all your loyalty cards in one place on your iPhone. Open the app at the till, hold up the barcode, and you're done. Everything stays on your device — no account, no sync, no servers.

## Requirements

- iPhone running iOS 17 or later
- Camera permission (only when scanning a barcode while adding a card)
- Photo Library permission (optional, only if attaching a custom logo)

## Privacy

Scandio doesn't collect, transmit, or share anything. The full [privacy policy](/scandio/privacy/) covers what's stored locally and how the optional Apple Intelligence category suggestion works.
