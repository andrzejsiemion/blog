## Theme

Switch between Light, Dark, and System (follow iPhone's appearance) under **Settings → Appearance**. The change is applied immediately.

The full-screen barcode view always uses Light mode internally, regardless of the app theme — that's intentional to keep the barcode against a bright white background for cashier scanners.

![Home grid in Dark theme](/scandio/manual/11-dark-mode.png)

## Language

English and Polish are bundled. Pick one in **Settings → Language**, then tap **Restart** in the prompt. Scandio quits and the next launch is in your chosen language.

If you prefer to follow iPhone's system language, leave the picker on the locale that matches your device — Scandio falls back to English for any system language other than Polish.
