If you enter a store name Scandio doesn't recognise, a **Category** section appears with twelve emoji choices:

🛒 grocery 🐕 pet 👕 clothing 💊 pharmacy ⛽ fuel 🪑 furniture ⚽ sports 🔨 DIY 📚 books 📺 electronics 👟 footwear 🏷️ other

Tap one to assign it as the card's icon.

## Apple Intelligence suggestion

On iPhone 15 Pro and newer running iOS 26+, Scandio asks the on-device language model to pick a category for you. You'll see one of the icons pre-selected with a blue ring — tap any other icon to override, or use **Clear** in the section header to remove it.

Nothing leaves your device — the suggestion runs entirely on Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework. See the [privacy policy](/scandio/privacy/#apple-intelligence-optional) for details.
