## Same barcode in multiple stores

Some chains share the same loyalty-card barcode across brands. Add the card under each brand name you'll use — either create them manually, or import a backup with **Add as New Cards** ([see Backup & Restore](../backup-and-restore/#duplicates)).

## Cashier rejects the scan

- Increase the brightness boost in **Settings → Barcode Display**.
- Wipe your screen — fingerprints scatter the laser.
- For EAN-13, EAN-8, and ITF, the cashier can also enter the number manually using the digits printed below the barcode in the [full-screen view](../showing-barcodes/).

## Switching iPhones

1. On the old phone: **Settings → Backup → Export All Cards**.
2. AirDrop or email the `.scandiobackup` file to the new phone.
3. Tap the file. Scandio opens with import already running.

Your favourites, tile colours, logos, and category icons all transfer.

## Restoring a deleted card

Deletes are immediate and permanent in the running app. To restore one, import your most recent `.scandiobackup` and pick **Add as New Cards** if the rest of your data is current, or **Merge with Existing** if you want every card overwritten by the backup.
