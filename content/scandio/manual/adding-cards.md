There are three ways to enter a card.

## Manual entry

1. Tap **+**.
2. **Card Name** — type the store's name (e.g. *Biedronka*, *IKEA*).
3. **Barcode Type** — Scandio auto-detects the type as you type the number. You can tap to change it.
4. **Card Number** — type or paste the number.
5. (Optional) **Tile Color** — pick a preset, or tap the rainbow swatch for a custom hex color.
6. **Save**.

For known store names, Scandio fills the brand colour and a built-in icon automatically (🐞 for Biedronka, 🛒 for Auchan, 💊 for Rossmann, 🪑 for IKEA, and many more).

![Add Card form with name, number, color and category](/scandio/manual/02-add-card.png)

## Scan an existing card

1. Tap **+** → **Scan Barcode with Camera**.
2. Hold your physical card so the barcode is centred in the camera view.
3. The number and barcode type fill in automatically.
4. Add a name and save.

## Photo logo

In the **Store Logo** section, tap **Choose from Photos** to pick an image from your library. Scandio resizes it to 256 px to keep things small.
