## What you'll need

- iPhone running **iOS 17 or later**
- Camera permission — only when you scan a barcode while adding a card
- Photo Library permission — optional, only if you want to attach a custom logo image

## First launch

When you open Scandio for the first time, the home screen is empty. Tap the **+** button in the top-right corner to add your first card.

![Empty state showing "No Cards Yet"](/scandio/manual/01-home-empty.png)

The next stop is [Adding Cards](../adding-cards/).
