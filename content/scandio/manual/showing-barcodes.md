---
title: "Showing Your Barcode"
weight: 40
---

Tap any card in the grid to display it full-screen for the cashier:

- **Card name** at the top
- The **barcode** in the middle, on a pure-white background for the cleanest scan
- The **card number** below, formatted in groups of 4 for easy manual entry
- **Barcode type** label at the bottom

Screen brightness automatically boosts to the level you set in [Settings](../settings/), then restores when you close the view. Tap the **X** in the top-right to close.

![Full-screen barcode display with name, code, and number](/scandio/manual/05-full-screen.png)

The full-screen view always uses Light mode internally, regardless of the app theme — that's intentional to keep the barcode against a bright white background for cashier scanners.
