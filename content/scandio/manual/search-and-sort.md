## Search

Pull down on the grid to reveal the search field, then type any part of a card name. The grid filters as you type.

![Searching the card grid](/scandio/manual/07-search.png)

## Sort

Open **Settings → Sort Cards By** and choose one:

- **Alphabetical** — by card name
- **Date Added** — newest first
- **Last Used** — most recently opened first

The sort applies immediately to both the home grid and the Quick Access carousel.
