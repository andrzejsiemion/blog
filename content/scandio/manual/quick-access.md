weight: 50
---

Mark cards you use often as favourites and they'll appear at the top of the home screen in a swipeable carousel.

- **Long-press** a card in the grid → **Add to Favorites**.
- Repeat to remove.

![Home screen showing the Quick Access carousel above the grid](/scandio/manual/06-home-grid.png)

The Quick Access carousel precaches its barcodes off the main thread, so opening a favourite card from the carousel is instant — there's no loading frame between the grid and the full-screen view.
